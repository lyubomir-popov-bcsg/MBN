What are toolbox dollars?

Toolbox dollars are available to help our business customers get even greater value from Tools powered by BCSG.
Business MasterCard cardholders that are eligible for Toolbox dollars can use them towards a monthly Tools powered by BCSG subscription, so you won’t be charged for the tools you subscribe to until your Toolbox dollars run out.
When they do eventually run out, we’ll charge the monthly subscription amount to your credit card.
One Toolbox dollar is worth one Canadian dollar, so you can easily work out how long they’ll last before your credit card is charged.

How can I get more Toolbox dollars?

We run regular campaigns that give Business MasterCard cardholders the opportunity to claim more Toolbox dollars. Just log in and check the ‘My payments’ page to see if you qualify.

Do Toolbox dollars expire?

Toolbox dollars need to be claimed within a certain time or they will expire. Different Toolbox dollars campaigns will have varying expiry dates. You can check your Toolbox dollars’ expiry date when you claim them on the ‘My payments’ page.
